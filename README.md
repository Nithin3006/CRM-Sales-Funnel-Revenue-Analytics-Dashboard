# CRM-Sales-Funnel-Revenue-Analytics-Dashboard
End-to-end CRM analytics project analyzing lead conversion, sales pipeline, and revenue performance using SQL and Power BI.

📌 Problem Statement

Businesses often struggle to track lead conversions, sales performance, and revenue generation across different stages of the sales pipeline.

🎯 Objective

To analyze CRM data and identify key insights to improve:

* Lead conversion rates
* Sales pipeline efficiency
* Revenue generation

🗂️ Dataset Description

The dataset consists of the following tables:

* **Leads** – potential customers
* **Accounts** – converted customers
* **Opportunities** – sales deals
* **Opportunity Products** – product-level revenue
* **Users** – sales representatives

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Excel – Initial data cleaning
* MySQL – Data querying & joins
* Python – Data preprocessing & EDA
* Power BI – Dashboard visualization

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

* Sales funnel analysis (Lead → Opportunity → Conversion)
* Conversion rate at each stage
* Revenue analysis by product and sales representative
* Lead source performance analysis
* Drop-off analysis in sales pipeline

## 📊 Key Insights

* Identified major drop-offs in the opportunity stage
* Certain lead sources show higher conversion rates
* Top sales reps contribute significantly to revenue
* Few products generate majority of revenue

## 🚀 Business Impact

* Helps businesses focus on high-performing lead sources
* Improves sales strategy by identifying bottlenecks
* Enables better decision-making for revenue growth
